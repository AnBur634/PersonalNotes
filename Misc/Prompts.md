

# Prompt structure
### Goal
What response do you want from AI?
### Context
Why do you need it and who is involved?
What is my role/ what is the role the AI should have when solving the problem?
### Source
Which information sources or samples should AI use?
### Expectations
How should AI respond to best meet your expectations

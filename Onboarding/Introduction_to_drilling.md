

\# Drilling

The drill string goes from the rig to the ground in order to transmit rotation force

string is turned by Kelly or top-drive

&nbsp;	string is made up of

&nbsp;		drill pipe

&nbsp;			2, 3 or 4 pipes are joint together to make a stand

&nbsp;		drill collar

&nbsp;		drill bit

drilling mud is pumped through the string

only can drill sedimentary rock as sandstone or shale

cannot drill through igneous or metamorphic rock such as granite, basalt

bottom hole assembly

&nbsp;	heavy weight drill pipe

Drill bit

&nbsp;	fixed cutter bit

&nbsp;	roller cone bit

BOP

&nbsp;	blow out preventer

&nbsp;	attached to wellhead

xmas tree

&nbsp;	sits on the wellhead

&nbsp;	controls the pressure of a production well

several casings are inserted into the ground

&nbsp;	conductor casing

 	surface casing

&nbsp;	intermediate casing

&nbsp;	production casing

drilling mud

&nbsp;	water based

&nbsp;	oil based

&nbsp;	containing additives to ensure i.e. certain viscosity suited for the well

&nbsp;	seals permeable layers of rock drilled into

&nbsp;	controls pressure in the hole as it prevents formation fluid from entering into the well
